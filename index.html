What is riso?

Risograph printing is a unique eco-friendly way of printing. Itâ€™s perfect for anything from art prints to flyers for your business. The way it works is not too far from the process of screen printing; your image is build up in layers of colours, sometimes running the paper through the machine multiple times to create the desired effect.

Equipment

We use a 2-colour Riso machine to print and currently hold 11 colours. We also have an electronic cutting machine, a folder-stapler & a wire-o binder.

Binding

There is a wide range of different binding options out there, for example sewn, tape and bound. We can help make them all happen may it be working with an external source or in-house

How to send it to us?

Each colour should be saved as a different gray-scale JPEG, with a minimum resolution of 300dpi (A3 size) We also accept PSD files in black & white, each layer being named appropriately
